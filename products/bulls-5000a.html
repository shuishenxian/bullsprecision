BULLS-5000A

MTP/MPO Multi-Fiber Connector Polishing Machine

The BULLS-5000A is our flagship polishing machine, purpose-built for MTP/MPO and other multi-fiber connectors. Featuring fully automated polishing control, precision pressure regulation, and multi-station simultaneous processing, it delivers consistently exceptional endface quality at scale.

Key Features

Advanced engineering for uncompromising polishing quality

Fully Automated Control

Programmable polishing recipes with one-touch operation. The intelligent control system manages pressure, speed, and time parameters automatically, minimizing operator skill dependency.

Precision Pressure Regulation

Closed-loop pneumatic pressure control ensures consistent downforce across all work stations. Real-time pressure monitoring with automatic compensation for stable polishing results.

Multi-Station Processing

Process up to 4 fixtures simultaneously with independent pressure control per station. Dramatically increases throughput without sacrificing quality or consistency.

High-Speed Production

Optimized polishing trajectories and rapid recipe switching enable faster cycle times. Designed for high-volume production environments requiring maximum uptime.

Intuitive Touch Interface

Large color touchscreen with graphical recipe editor. Store up to 200 polishing programs with easy import/export via USB for quick setup across multiple machines.

Built for Reliability

Industrial-grade components with robust mechanical design ensure long service life. Low maintenance requirements with easy-access consumable replacement for minimal downtime.
