BULLS-LS1000

Precision Fiber Laser Cutting Machine

The BULLS-LS1000 utilizes advanced fiber laser technology to deliver ultra-precise fiber cutting with exceptionally smooth, flat cut surfaces. Equipped with an automatic feeding system, it ensures high throughput and repeatability for demanding production environments.

Key Features

Laser precision for flawless fiber cutting results

Laser Precision Cutting

CO2 or fiber laser source delivers clean, precise cuts with minimal heat-affected zone. Achieves endface angles below 0.5 degrees for superior downstream polishing results.

Automatic Feeding System

Motorized fiber feeding mechanism with programmable cut lengths ensures consistent results. Continuous operation mode enables unattended batch processing.

Programmable Cut Length

Precisely set fiber cut lengths with micrometer accuracy. Digital length control eliminates manual measurement errors and ensures batch-to-batch consistency.

Multi-Fiber Compatibility

Handles single-mode, multi-mode, PM fiber, specialty fibers, and fiber ribbons. Quick-change fiber holders accommodate various fiber diameters and coatings.

Safety Enclosure

Fully enclosed Class 1 laser safety design with interlock switches. Integrated fume extraction protects operators and maintains a clean working environment.

Intelligent Control Interface

Touchscreen interface with real-time process monitoring, cut count tracking, and maintenance alerts. Store and recall cutting programs for different fiber types.
